    Unassigned:Here Admins and Managers will receive all raised tickets, from here they can assign tickets to agents and supervisor.

    Team Tickets: Here Agents and Supervisors can see tickets those assigned to them and their team members.

    Teams ResolvedHere Agents or Supervisors can see tickets those tickets have been resolved (Ticket’s status is updated to resolved.

    Teams Closed Ticket: Here Agents or Supervisors can see tickets those tickets have been closed. (Ticket’s status is updated to closed)

    Ticket Mailbox: The tickets raised from help desk email ID will fall under this, Admin can view such tickets. You can give access to view ticket mailbox to Ticket Mail Manager from settings.

    My Tickets: Here agent or supervisor can see tickets only when assigned to them.

    Requested by MeAdmin, Manager, supervisor, and agents can see their raised tickets in this tab.

    My ResolvedAdmin, Manager, supervisor, and agents can see their tickets those have been resolved.

    Note: Admin can enable or disable this tab from custom settings.

    My ClosedAdmin, Manager, supervisor, and agents can see their tickets those have been closed

    Importance of Roles: Admin: • Admin has full control. • Able to do all settings. • Able to raise tickets behalf of another person. • Can generate reports of all teams • Can view dashboard • Able to assign the tickets of all teams • Can view all the teams in side navigation

    Supervisor: • Able to raise tickets behalf of another person. • Receives only raised ticket to their department. • Able to assign the tickets based on ticket scopes • Can generate reports of their teams • Can view dashboard • Can view only those teams in side navigation if they are supervisors of those teams (teams specific)

    Agents: • Able to raise tickets behalf of another person. • They can be able to change the status of their assign tickets and can be able to re-assign to others. • Able to assign the tickets based on ticket scopes

    Note: Based on custom settings, admin, supervisor, and agents can delete the tickets
