              Users


              Admin can assign roles to O365 users with ticket scope as per requirement in Help Desk application. You can select both supervisor and agents for multiple teams but ticket scope will remain same and roles one.


              What would happen if user role is agent and he/she is supervisor of team?

              They will receive notification as a part of supervisor of teams according to Email notification settings and perform activities as per agent roles.

                Importance of Roles:

              Admin:
            • Admin has full control.
            • Able to do all settings.
            • Able to raise tickets behalf of another person.
            • Can generate reports of all teams.
            • Can view dashboard.
            • Able to assign the tickets of all teams.
            • Can view all the teams in side navigation.

              • Supervisor:
            • Able to raise tickets behalf of another person.
            • Receives only raised ticket to their department.
            • Able to assign the tickets based on ticket scopes.
            • Can generate reports of their teams.
            • Can view dashboard.
            • Can view only those teams in side navigation if they are supervisors of those teams (teams specific).

              • Agents:
            • Able to raise tickets behalf of another person.
            • They can be able to change the status of their assign tickets and can be able to re-assign to others.
            • Able to assign the tickets based on ticket scopes

              • Based on custom settings, admin, supervisor, and agents can delete the tickets.
