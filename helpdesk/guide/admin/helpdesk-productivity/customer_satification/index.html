          Customer Satisfaction

            A survey link is sent to the requester once the ticket has been resolved or closed. From here admin can create survey question for resolved or closed tickets. You can add additional survey question from additional survey questions link . if you select “Allow agent s to send survey link” option that would be visible in comments page link will send to requester once we click on it .If you want to disable the survey link, edit the survey, disable the toggle switch and click on submit button.
