    Roles

    The Recruitment Management 365 application supports different user roles, each with specific access and responsibilities.

    1. Approver
    2. Recruiter
    3. Recruiter Manager
    4. Interviewer
    5. Screener
    6. Hiring Manager

    Approver

    Users with the Approver role can access the My Approvals tab directly from the Home Page. This section lists all requisitions that require their review and approval.

    Approvers can view requisitions and take actions such as Approve or Reject. They cannot view job posts or create them.


    Selecting a requisition opens the Requisition Approval Form, where approvers can review all details.


    Approvers can take the required action by selecting either Approve or Reject.



    Recruiter

    The Recruiter role has one of the most comprehensive permission sets. Recruiters can view requisitions, view job posts, and create job posts. They manage the full recruitment process, including creating requisitions, posting jobs, reviewing candidates, and tracking progress.


    Recruiters have access to most features available to Admins, except configuration-related options. For administrative controls, please refer to the Admin Guide.

    Recruiter Manager

    The Recruiter Manager has permissions similar to Recruiters, including:

    • Viewing requisitions
    • Viewing job posts
    • Creating job posts

    Recruiter Managers typically oversee hiring operations and ensure job postings and requisitions follow the correct approval flow. They can also view the approval flow of requisitions but cannot modify the workflow.


    Interviewer

    The Interviewer participates in the interview process. Based on the permission table, Interviewers can:

    • View job posts relevant to their assigned interviews
    • CANNOT view requisitions
    • CANNOT create job posts

    Interviewers record feedback and evaluation results for candidates during their assigned interview rounds.



    Screener

    A Screener is responsible for reviewing and shortlisting sourced candidates. According to the permission table, Screeners:

    • CANNOT view requisitions
    • May view assigned job posts (depending on selectors)
    • CANNOT create job posts


    Hiring Manager

    A Hiring Manager plays a key role in reviewing and selecting candidates. Based on the permission table, Hiring Managers:

    • Can view requisitions
    • Can view job posts
    • Can create job posts

    They review shortlisted profiles, provide feedback, collaborate with recruiters, and make final hiring decisions.
