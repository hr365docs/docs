Job Posting

You can add job postings Values here.


    Job Location

    Click the “+Add Job Location” button to create a new job location entry.



    Fill out the location details and click “Save” to complete the process.



    If available, use system integration tools to sync locations from external sources.



    Previously added job locations appear in a dropdown menu for easy selection.



    Bulk deactivation becomes available when multiple locations are selected.



    Inactive locations are listed separately and can be reviewed or reactivated.



    To restore a location, check the box beside it and enable it again.



    Reactivated locations will reappear under the active section.



    While adding a location, a toggle lets users assign it as active or inactive. Inactive entries will appear in the respective section.



    Departments

    Click the “+Add” button to add a new department.



    Enter department details such as name and remarks, then save the entry.



    Admins can select multiple departments and bulk mark them as inactive.



    Inactive departments will be listed separately and can be managed accordingly.



    To restore a department, tick its checkbox and activate it again.



    Reactivated departments will appear back in the active list.



    During department creation, a toggle allows you to set its initial status as active or inactive. Inactive entries will be shown in the dedicated section.



    Job Priority

    Click the “+Add Job Priority” button to define a new priority level for job listings.



    Fill out the required fields and click “Save” to store the new priority level.



    All added priorities are available for selection via a dropdown menu and can influence recruitment workflows accordingly.



    When multiple priority entries are selected, a “Set Bulk Inactive” action becomes available.



    Inactive priorities appear in the dedicated Inactive section for management.



    If a priority was marked inactive accidentally, it can be re-enabled by selecting the corresponding checkbox.



    Reactivated priorities are listed again in the Active section.



    During setup, a toggle allows the user or administrator to assign the initial status. If turned off, the entry will be added as inactive.



    Job Source

    Click the “+Add Job Source” button to define a new source for job listings.



    Fill out the details and click “Save” to create the job source.



    To deactivate a source, check the box beside the entry you want to disable.



    Inactive sources will be listed in the dedicated section for visibility.



    To reactivate a source, check the box and enable it again.



    Reactivated entries will appear under the active section.



    Use the active/inactive toggle during setup to define the initial status.



    Job Title

    Click the “+Add Job Title” button to introduce a new role designation.



    Complete the form and click “Save” to register the job title.



    Use integration options to sync titles from other systems if applicable.



    Added titles are available via dropdown selection within relevant forms.



    Deactivated titles will appear in the inactive section for review.



    To reactivate, select the checkbox next to the title and enable it.



    The restored title will reappear in the active list.



    During setup, toggle the job title status between active and inactive as needed.



    Job Type

    Click the “+Add” button to create a new job type category.



    Fill out the required details and click “Save” to store the job type.



    All added job types are available in dropdown menus across related sections.



    Users can select multiple job types and mark them as inactive if needed.



    Inactive job types appear under the designated Inactive tab.



    To reactivate a job type, check the associated box and confirm the change.



    The job type will then reappear under the active list.



    During setup, a toggle allows users to set the initial status—active or inactive. By default, job types are added as active unless the toggle is turned off.



    Job Code

    Configure how job codes are generated and applied throughout the system.


    Enable Prefix Text: When activated, a predefined prefix is automatically added to each job code—useful for organizing codes by department, region, or business logic.


    Enable Free Text: Allows users to manually enter custom job codes instead of relying on system-generated formats.

    After selecting your preferred settings, click Save to apply the changes.


    Work Mode

    Click the “+Add” button to create a new work mode entry.



    Fill in the required details and click “Save” to confirm the addition.



    Added work modes are available in dropdown menus and affect job postings where applicable.



    Multiple work modes can be selected and marked as inactive by the user or admin.



    Inactive entries are displayed under the designated tab.



    To reactivate, check the box next to a work mode and confirm the change.



    The work mode will then reappear in the active list.



    During creation, a toggle lets users assign the initial status of the work mode. It defaults to active unless switched off.
