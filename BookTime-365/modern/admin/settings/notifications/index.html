Notifications


Manage and configure notifications for clients and staff to ensure timely updates about appointments, changes, and system events. This section enables customization for both email templates and shared mailbox configurations.


Email Template

Customize the default templates for emails sent to clients and staff.


You can edit the subject, body, and placeholders to personalize the message. Additionally, you can add CC recipients for each template. Once changes are made, click the "Update" button to save.


Shared Mailbox

Configure a shared mailbox to centralize outgoing and incoming notifications. This helps ensure that all notification emails are managed through a single, monitored email account.
