    Roles

    The HRMS-HR365 application supports different user roles, each with specific access and responsibilities.

    1. Approver
    2. Recruiter
    3. Recruiter Manager
    4. Interviewer
    5. Screener
    6. Hiring Manager

    Approver

    Users with the Approver role can access the My Approvals tab directly from the Home Page. This section lists all requisitions that require their review and approval. Each entry provides essential details.

    Approvers can take action directly from this view, making the approval process seamless and efficient.


    Clicking on the job title within the My Approvals section opens the Requisition Approval Form, providing detailed information about the request.



    Within the form, approvers have the option to take appropriate action by selecting either Approve or Reject.




    Recruiter

    The Recruiter view provides tools to manage the full recruitment lifecycle. From creating requisitions to posting jobs and scheduling interviews, recruiters can access all key features in one place.


    Recruiters have access to nearly all the same functionalities as Admin users, except for settings-related options. For configuration and administrative tasks, please refer to the Admin Guide.

    Recruiter Manager

    The Recruiter Manager has access to manage job postings within their department. They can create requisitions and post jobs specific to their team's hiring needs.

    Both Recruiter and Recruiter Manager can view the approval flow of requisitions but do not have permissions to create or modify approval workflows.


    Interviewer

    The Interviewer participates in the recruitment process by evaluating candidates during interviews. They are assigned to specific interview rounds and can record feedback for each candidate.


    Screener

    A Screener is responsible for filtering sourced candidates. They review the applications they are assigned to, shortlist suitable profiles, and move candidates to the next stage of the recruitment process.


    Hiring Manager

    Any user within the organization who is designated as a Hiring Manager can oversee the recruitment process for their assigned open positions. They collaborate with recruiters, interviewers, and screeners to ensure the right candidate is selected. As part of their role, they review shortlisted profiles, provide feedback, and make final hiring decisions.
