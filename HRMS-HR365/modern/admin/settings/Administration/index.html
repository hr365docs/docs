    Administration

    The Administration section is used to set up and manage the core structural elements of the organization within the HRMS system. It includes key configurations such as departments, organizational hierarchy, job grades, locations, and positions. This section helps in maintaining consistent organizational data across various modules of the application.


    Department

    Departments: Define and manage various departments across the organization. This ensures proper alignment of users and teams within functional groups.


    To add a new department, click on the “+ Add” button located at the top of the Departments panel. A form will appear on the right side where you can enter the department name, such as Sales. You can choose to activate the department immediately by toggling the Active switch. Once all details are filled in, click the Save button to create and store the department in the system.


    Use “Sync from M365” to import departments directly from Microsoft 365. This keeps your department list up to date with your organization’s M365 structure, reducing manual entry.



    The Bulk Delete option allows you to select and remove multiple departments at once, making it easy to clean up outdated or unused entries efficiently.



    Organization

    The Organization setup defines the hierarchical structure of the company. It allows you to map how different departments or units are structured within the organization, supporting multi-level reporting lines and organizational views.

    To add a new organization, click the "+ Add" button. Under the General tab, enter the organization's basic information.


    In the Bank Details tab, fill in the organization's banking information for financial transactions.


    The Intermediate Bank Details section is used for international transactions that involve a secondary (intermediate) bank. This is typically required when the primary bank does not have a direct connection with the receiving bank.



    The Terms and Conditions tab allows you to define any legal, policy, or compliance-related statements relevant to the organization.



    Badges

    Badges: Manage digital badges or recognitions that may be awarded to users for achievements, certifications, or milestones.


    Click the + Add button to create a new badge.
    Enter the badge name, add a description, toggle it active if needed, and click Save.


    Grade

    Grades: Set up and maintain employee grade levels that determine compensation bands, job levels, and organizational role tiers.


    Click the + Add button to create a new grade.
    Enter the grade name and click Save to add it to the system.



    Location

    Location: Add and manage geographic locations or office branches where employees are assigned.


    Click the + Add button to create a new location.
    Enter the location name, add a description, and toggle Active if required.



    Click Sync From M365 to automatically fetch and update the latest office locations from your Microsoft 365 directory.


    Position

    Position: Define job positions or titles available within the company and associate them with departments and grades for structured role assignment.


    Click the + Add button to define a new job position.
    Enter the position title, provide details, and click Save.



    Click Sync From M365 to import job positions directly from the Microsoft 365 directory to keep your position list updated.



Delegation


The Delegation feature in HRMS-HR365 allows administrators to temporarily assign their responsibilities to another user, ensuring business continuity when they are unavailable.


To create a new delegation, click on the + Add button.


Administrators can create delegations for any user and assign delegatees, while standard users can only create delegations for themselves and choose a delegatee from the list defined by administrators.


From the user list, choose the delegatee who will temporarily handle the responsibilities.


Specify the start date and end date for the delegation period.


Select the specific application for which you want to create a Delegation.


Use the Status toggle to activate or deactivate the delegation. Once all details are entered, click Add to finalize the delegation.
