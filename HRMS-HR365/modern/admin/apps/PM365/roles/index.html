  • Roles

      The performance management tool comes with multi-role functionalities.

      • User
      • HR Executive
      • HR Manager
      • HR Head
      • HR Manager-Admin
      • HR Head-Admin
      • Admin

  • User


      The user could perform self-review during their appraisal cycle.

      The user could request 360 feedback from other users during their appraisal cycle.

      The user could provide feedback on other user's 360 feedback requests.

  • Manager (Employee’s Manager)


      • Managers could perform his/her self-review during their appraisal cycle.
      • They can request 360 feedbacks from other users during their appraisal cycle.
      • They could provide feedback on other user's 360 feedback requests.
      • Managers can do appraisal reviews of their team members upon a team member completes its self-review.
      • They can insert additional reviews to their appraisees (team members).


  • HR Executive


      • They can perform their self-review at the time of the appraisal cycle.
      • May request 360 feedbacks from other users at their appraisal cycle.
      • They can provide feedback on other user's 360 feedback requests.
      • Perform HR review on the employees after the manager has completed the review.
      • Extend self-review days on employee's requests.
      • 360 feedback for employees from their team.
      • Request organization feedback.
      • Access or review the report page.


  • HR Manager


      • They can perform their self-review at the appraisal cycle
      • 360 feedback could be requested from other users at their appraisal cycle
      • Provide feedback on other user's 360 feedback requests
      • Perform HR review on the employees after the manager has completed the review.
      • Extend self-review days on employee's requests.
      • 360 feedback for employees from their team.
      • Request organization feedback.
      • Access or review the report page.
      • They can view or access administration settings.


  • HR Head


      • They can perform their self-review at the appraisal cycle.
      • 360 feedback could be requested from other users at their appraisal cycle.
      • Provide feedback on other user's 360 feedback requests.
      • Perform HR review on the employees after the manager has completed the review.
      • Extend self-review days on employee's requests.
      • 360 feedback for employees from their team.
      • Request organization feedback.
      • Access or review the report page.
      • They can view or access administration settings.
      • They can view or access the setting.
      • The final release of the appraisal to the employees, whose reviews (User, Manager, and HR reviews ) are complete


  • HR Manager Admin


      • They can perform their self-review at the appraisal cycle.
      • 360 feedback could be requested from other users at their appraisal cycle.
      • Provide feedback on other user's 360 feedback requests.
      • Perform HR review on the employees after the manager has completed the review.
      • Extend self-review days on employee's requests.
      • 360 feedback for employees from their team.
      • Request organization feedback.
      • Access or review the report page.
      • They can view or access administration settings.
      • They can view or access the setting.
