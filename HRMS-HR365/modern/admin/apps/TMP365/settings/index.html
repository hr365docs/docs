General

Here, you can change theme, date format, color code, brand logo etc.



  1. Annual Leave Start Date and Annual Leave End Date
    You can specify the annual leave start and end dates here, or alternatively, set them based on the joining date, depending on your requirements for leave calculation. If accrued leaves are enabled, leaves would be calculated and allocated to users based on these date and date of joining of the candidate.




    Example
    let say
    candidate joining date = 15 th March 2024.
    Annual Start date= 1 st Jan 2024
    Annual End Date = 31st Dec 2024.
    Casual Leaves = 24.

    ✔ If accrued is disabled, candidate allocated leave would be (24 leaves /12 month) * Remaining months i.e., 9 months = 18 Leaves allocates for a years

    ✔ If accrued is enabled and type is Monthly, for march month allocated leave will be 2 and it increase by 2 on every month.

    ✔ If accrued is enabled and type is quarterly
    24 leaves/ 3 quarter for year = 8 leaves for each quarter
    Now (1st quarter= 8 leaves) / 4 months = 2 leaves
    ∴ 2 leaves * 2 months (remaining months to complete one quarter) = 4 leaves allocates in first quarter and then 8 leaves would allocate for in each quarter automatically.

Advance




  1. Leave accrual
    By enabling this option, users will have their leaves accrued every month rather than allotted on the first day of the year.



  2. HR approvers
    You can add an HR Approver here.



  3. Additional approvers
    Give applicants the option to choose another approver(s) in case their first-line manager is unavailable.



  4. Auto Approvals.
    leaves can approve automattically without permission.



    5. Auto approval of first approvers:Enable automatic approval for specific leave types and/or job titles at the first level.

    Auto approval of secondary approvers:Implement automatic approval for designated leave types and/or job titles at the second level.

    Auto approval of tertiary approvers:Third level auto approval of selected leave types and/or selected job titles

    Auto approval exception:Please specify the name of the leave type(s) that you do not want to be automatically approved.

  5. Second level approval for leave cancellation.
    HR is Second level approver for Leave Cancellation.




  6. Select the leave type to carry forward
    At the end of the year, one of the leave types can be carried forward to the next year.




  7. Add Comp off (off in lieu) policy (in days)
    Comp (Compensatory) Off leave (also called compensatory off, comp off, or off-In-Lieu) allows team members to take time off in lieu of overtime pay if they've worked under irregular circumstances, such as: when business is closed, holidays, overtime hours, etc. Users are allowed to consume their comp off with X days from the day they worked under irregular circumstances.



  8. Avail Comp off (off in lieu) policy (in days)
    Comp (Compensatory) Off leave (also called compensatory off, comp off, or off-In-Lieu) allows team members to take time off in lieu of overtime pay if they've worked under irregular circumstances, such as: when business is closed, holidays, overtime hours, etc. Users are allowed to consume their comp off with X days from the day they worked under irregular circumstances.

    You can allow external users to use this Time-off Manager app i.e. You can add them as guest / external user which is added in your office 365 tenant

    By enabling this feature, approved leave requests will create events in the Outlook calendars of the selected roles.
    Note: This requires the Power Automate flow integration.



  11. Show Time off requests in Microsoft outlook calender.

    If enabled, you can find control of recurrence on the top right corner after clicking on 'new request' on home page of the application

    If enabled, the 'Team Approvals' section will be visible on the home page, allowing higher-level managers (managers of managers) to review and act on team requests.

Audit Logs

The Audit Logs feature in the application provides a detailed record of all user activities and system changes within the Settings module. It helps administrators track modifications, identify unauthorized actions, and ensure compliance with organizational policies.

Click on this icon button.



After clicking, a panel will be shown where you can check the history of any setting.



If the user wants to view the previous history of the Additional Approvers, they should click on the Additional Approvers option. Both the previous and current values will be displayed, showing what it was before and what changes have been made.



Then, the Audit Logs panel will be displayed. The user should click on the Additional Approvers option.



Then, the user will get all the details of the Audit Logs.
