  • Home Page

      • The 1:1 365 Home Page provides employees, team leads, and managers with a structured interface to schedule, conduct, and track 1:1 365 meetings. It is designed to boost productivity, accountability, and follow-up tracking within your organization.


    Navigate between Meetings, My Tasks to access personalized and team-level meeting insights.


    1:1 365 Templates: You can create reusable templates to standardize the structure of your one-on-one meetings.

    Click on the 1:1 365 Template option to view existing templates and manage them.


    To create a new template, click on the + New button.


    Fill in the required fields with your template details (such as title, agenda points, or structure), and then click the Create button.


    Once created, the template will appear in the 1:1 365 Template panel. From here, you can edit the template to update details or delete it if no longer needed.


    Adding a New Meeting: Click + New to open the scheduling panel.


    Enter a meeting title, pick a template or custom agenda, choose participants, set the time.


    Recurrence Pattern: After selecting the start date and time, click the Recurrence checkbox to schedule repeating 1:1 365 meetings automatically. You can set the frequency to Weekly, Monthly, or Quarterly, and define the range of recurrence by setting start and end dates.


    add talking points, action points if you want to add new point section click on + icon.



    Once all details are entered (including recurrence if needed), click Send to schedule the meeting.


    Actions: After scheduling a meeting, you can easily manage it using the available options. You can edit a meeting to update its details such as time, participants, or agenda, or delete it if the meeting is no longer required.


    Settings: Administrators can configure options such as theme (Light, Dark, Site), response visibility (anonymous or public), and permissions for who can create or vote in polls.


    Help: The Help panel provides license details, version information, subscription validity, and quick links to Support, Privacy Policy, FAQ, Release Notes, Product Activation, and License Validation.
