• New Subscription in Azure: Creating a Pay-As-You-Go Subscription


    1. Sign in to the Azure Portal
      Go to the Azure Portal and sign in with your Microsoft account.

    2. Navigate to Subscriptions In the Azure portal, navigate to the "Subscriptions" section using the search bar or from the menu.


    3. Add a New Subscription

      4. Click the "Add" button to create a new subscription.



    4. Fill in Subscription Details
      On the “Create a subscription” page, under the “Basics” tab:
      1. Subscription Name: Enter a name for your subscription.
      2. Billing Account: Select the billing account where the new subscription will be created.
      3. Billing Profile: Choose the billing profile for the subscription.
      4. Invoice Section: Select the invoice section for the subscription.
      5. Select the Plan: Next to “Plan,” select “Add a different type of subscription.”


    5. This action will open a new tab in your browser. In the new tab, click “OK” to proceed.


    6. Choose Pay-As-You-Go Offer
      1. From the list of offers, locate Pay-As-You-Go.
      2. Click on “Select offer” next to Pay-As-You-Go to move to the next step.


    7. Accept Terms and Provide Details
      1. Select the checkbox to agree to the terms.
      2. Click “Next” to continue.


    8. Enter Required Information
      Provide the necessary details, including:

      1. PAN ID
      2. Card details
      3. Any other requested information as shown in the screenshots.












    9. Select Support Plan
      1. When prompted for technical support options, select “No technical support” as highlighted in the screenshot.
      2. Click on “Sign up” to complete the subscription setup.


    10. Verify New Subscription
      1. After completing the signup, navigate to the Home page in the Azure portal.
      2. Click on Subscriptions.
      3. You should now see the new subscription listed under the name you created.


    11. Retrieve the Billing Account ID
      1. Click on the subscription you just created to open its details.
      2. Go to Settings and then Properties.
      3. Scroll down to locate the Billing Account ID.
      4. Copy this Billing Account ID and send it to us. This will allow us to create a personalized private offer for you.
