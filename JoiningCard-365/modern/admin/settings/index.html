Settings

The JoiningCard 365 Settings panel allows administrators to configure essential features such as theme preferences, user management, SharePoint visibility, and card customization. These options help tailor the user experience, enhance branding consistency, and provide a seamless onboarding interface for employees.

Theme

Choose a preferred visual theme for JoiningCard 365 to align with your SharePoint site’s style or your organization’s branding. Users can switch between light, dark, or site-based themes to improve the overall aesthetic and readability.

  • Light Mode: Offers a bright, clean layout for better visibility in well-lit environments.


  • Dark Mode: Ideal for low-light settings, providing a modern dark interface to reduce eye strain.


  • Site Mode: Inherits your current SharePoint site’s theme to ensure design consistency.

Hide SharePoint Settings

The Hide SharePoint Settings option allows administrators to disable SharePoint’s default menu within the JoiningCard 365 environment. This feature helps maintain focus on onboarding content and prevents unauthorized modifications to SharePoint site settings.



Users

The Users section allows administrators to manage access roles and permissions for JoiningCard 365. Admins can add, edit, or remove users to ensure the right people have access to configuration settings.



  • Add New User: Click the +Add button, fill in the user details, assign a role (Admin or User), and click Save.



  • Edit or Delete: Use the respective icons under the “Actions” column to modify or remove users.



  • Search & Pagination: Search by name or email and control the number of entries displayed per page (10, 25, or 50).

Customize Card

The Customize Card section lets administrators personalize the onboarding card displayed on the JoiningCard 365 wall. You can enable or disable the card slider, set the slider timing, and create a custom welcome message for new employees.

  • Step 1: Click Configure under Customize Card in the Settings panel.



  • Step 2: Toggle Enable Card Slider to activate the feature.



  • Step 3: Adjust the Slider Timer using the range slider to control how quickly cards rotate.



  • Step 4: Edit the Welcome Message to greet new members with a custom note. Keep it short, warm, and professional.
