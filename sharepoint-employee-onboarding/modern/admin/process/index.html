Onboarding Process


  • In the Onboarding tab, you add new or existing employee details.



    • Step 1: Adding employee

    Add

    1. Click on the +New, this displays Onboard page. In Basic information tab, fill the basic details of the new joining candidate and click Save & Continue button.


      New Joinee
      For adding new joinee, follow the below steps:

      1. In confidential Details tab, fill the confidentail details of the new joining candidate and click Save & Continue button.


      2. In Work Details tab, fill the work details of the new joining candidate and click Save & Continue button.


      3. In Task tab, select the task template and click Submit button.


      Existing
      For adding existing employee, follow the below steps:

      1. Click on the + Existing, this displays Existing page. Select Employee Name from M365.

        2. Once you add Employee Name, all details will fetch from M365.

      Bulk Add

        Download the sample file and fill the details as per specified format in the sample and save it. Choose the same file and click on Save button.



      2. Step 2: Task details

    2. Click on the employee name, that displays Employee details.



    3. Manage the task by updating the status by selecting open, In Progress, Completed or Abandoned.

Offboarding Process


    In the Offboarding tab, you add employee details and task.


    For adding offboard employee details, follow the below steps:

    1. Click on the +Add, this displays Offboard page. In Basic information tab, search the employee name from HR365 and click Save & Continue button.



    2. In Tasks tab, select the Task Templates and click Submit button.

Onboarded


    In the onboarded tab, you can see all the onboarded employees. You can upload documents, search teams, add confidential details and work details.



    1. Click on the Onborded tab, that displays Joining Formalities. From here you can upload documents.



    2. Click on Teams tab, from here you can search teams.



    3. Click on Work Details, fill the field and click on Save button.
