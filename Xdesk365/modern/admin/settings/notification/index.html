    Notification

    From here admin can edit the email notification body or can disable the email notification.
    Different notifications are sent to admin, supervisor, assignee, and requesters based on different actions on tickets as per email notification settings.

    Requester

    The admin can edit the email notification body in email templates and can disable or stop notifications that are send to the requester. To disable the notification, open the template, uncheck the Active check box and click on the Save button. Following are the different actions on the tickets that leads in sending email notifications to the requester.

    • When a new ticket is created by the requester.
    • When tickets have been assigned or resolved or closed.
    • When agents comments on the tickets.
    • When tickets transfer from one department to another.
    • When a ticket splits into another ticket.
    • When the requester's ticket is merged with another ticket.
    • When a sub ticket is created from the main ticket.


    Assignee

    The assignee (admin supervisor and agents) are the one who gets the ticket assigned. Admin could edit the email notification body in email templates and disable or stop notifications sent to the assignee. To disable the notification, open the template, uncheck the Active check box and click on the Save button. Following are the different actions on the tickets that lead to sending email notifications to the assignee.

    • When tickets have been assigned.
    • When private comments by others.
    • When the requester's ticket is merged.
    • When a ticket is modified by others.


    Supervisor

    Admin could edit the email notification body in email templates and can disable or stop notifications sent to the supervisor. To disable the notification, open the template, uncheck the Active check box and click on the Save button. Following are the different actions on the tickets that lead to sending email notifications to the supervisor.

    • When a new ticket creates.
    • When a sub ticket is created from the main ticket.


    Admin

    When a new ticket is created, a notification is sent to the admin. To disable the notification, open the template, uncheck the Active check box, and click on the Save button.


    Approver

    Approvers are responsible for reviewing and approving tickets. Admins can edit the email notification content in email templates and enable or disable notifications sent to approvers. To disable the notification, open the template, uncheck the Active checkbox, and click the Save button. The following actions on tickets trigger email notifications to the approvers:


    Disable Default Approval Email to Admins: This option allows you to disable approval notifications for the default admin. Once enabled, the admin will no longer receive ticket approval alerts.



    When this toggle is enabled, approval emails will be sent exclusively from the users specified within the application. If the toggle is disabled, approval emails will be sent from both the application users and the site users, ensuring broader distribution.



    Editing Approver Email Notification Templates: When you click the edit icon in the Approver section, the corresponding email notification template opens for customization.




    In this editor, you can modify the Email Subject and Email Body fields, specify recipients in the To and CC fields, and enable or disable the template using the Active checkbox. Once the necessary changes are made, click Save to apply the updates.
