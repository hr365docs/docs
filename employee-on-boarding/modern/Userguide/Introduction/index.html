Introduction

HR365 Employee Onboarding experience is a pleasure experience for new joinee as well as no burden on HR team. With the help of HR365 On-boarding app, no more paper work, employees integrate into their teams quicker. HR365 stores all the important HR documents such as offer letters, employee credentials, employee handbook online in Office 365’s secure storage, hence eliminates the need of storing confidential data on any third party servers.
