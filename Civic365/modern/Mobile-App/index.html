  • Android App

      1. To get the Civic365 app, just head to the Google Play Store or Apple App Store on your phone and search for "Civic365 Canada".


      2. To initiate the installation of the Civic365 application, simply click on the "Install" button.


      3. Select the Town Innisfil.


      4. If you already have an account, please log in. If you haven't registered yet, click on the "Register" button and proceed with the registration process.


      5. To create an account, enter your details as depicted in the image and then click on the "Create Account" button.


      6. After creating your account, please check your Outlook inbox for temporary credentials.


      7. Enter your email address and password.



      8. Please Modify the password.


      9. To share your device's location, select the appropriate option.


      10. Enter the location you desire into the search bar to initiate the request.


      11. Choose the category that corresponds to the issue you encountered.


      12. Choose the Sub category that corresponds to the issue you encountered.

      13. Please provide a description of your request and select an image to accompany it.

      14. Once you've attached the image, click on the proceed button to continue.


      15. This is what your request looks like.

      16. Submit Your Request.

      17. You can view all your requests in the request window.

      18. In your profile, you can view your reported or updated issues, just as you see your profile.

      19. In the Update window, you can view updates.

      20. In the Manage Account window, you can delete or modify your account.

      21. If you encounter any issues, you can report them by emailing us through the contact information provided on the help page.

      22. In the "About" section, you can find information about the app version or the Android version

      23. In the "Notification" section, you can find updates.

      24. In the request detail section, you can view comments and all the details of your reported request.
