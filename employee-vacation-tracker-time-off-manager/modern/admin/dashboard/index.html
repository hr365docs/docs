Dashboard

This dashboard serves as a centralized hub for monitoring and analyzing employee time-off patterns across departments and locations in real-time.



Time-off Type by Department

Illustrates the distribution of different types of time-off (Comp Off, Out of Office, Professional Leave, PTO, Sick, Vacation) across various departments. Helps identify trends in absence behavior and supports better workforce planning.


Time-off Type by Location

Shows how different types of time-off are distributed across organizational locations. Facilitates location-based workforce management and identifies regions with higher absence rates.


Absence Rate by Department

Displays the overall absence rate percentage for each department, providing insight into department-level attendance trends and potential areas for intervention.


Absence Rate by Location

Shows the overall absence rate percentage for each location. Enables targeted management strategies for locations with higher absenteeism.


Weekdays Time-off by Location

Displays the number of time-off occurrences for each weekday by location. Supports scheduling decisions and helps minimize operational disruptions during peak absence days.
