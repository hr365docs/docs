• Configuring Power Automate to Send Birthday and Work Anniversary Emails

    Prerequisites:
    1. We need M365 group that includes all users from the Employee Directory.

    2. The person who configured Power Automate must have a Power Automate license.
    Steps to configure the Power Automate flow
    1. Sign in to portal.office.com, then access the app launcher and choose 'Admin'


    2. You will now be directed to the Microsoft 365 admin centre. From here, select 'Active teams & groups' under ‘Teams & Groups’ and by clicking 'Add a M365 group' provide the required information and include all users listed in the Employee Directory and then save your settings.


    3. Now, select 'Power Automate' from the app launcher.


    4. Go to the left panel and choose 'My flows’. Import the solution at the top by uploading the downloaded file. To download the Power Automate solution, please click Here Employee Directory 365. Note: Do not unzip the solution file


    5. Click the upload button to select and import the downloaded file.


    6. Choose "Create as New" in the setup dropdown.
