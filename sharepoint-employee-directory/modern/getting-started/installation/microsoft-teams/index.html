  • Installation of Employee Directory 365 from Microsoft Teams


      1. Once the application has been deployed, choose it and select the Sync to Teams option from the files menu.


      2. Go to the Sharepoint site's side navigation and select Teams.


      3. Teams will open and now click on Apps from side navigation. Click on the Employee Directory 365 application as shown below in the image.


      4. A dialog box will appear, select Add to a team from the dropdown.


      5. Employee Directory 365 will be available for the entire team,but start by setting it up in a channel.


      6. Choose the channel for the application you wish to setup. After choosing a channel, click on Set Up a Tab button.


      7. Now click on the Save button.


      8. Employee Directory 365 application will be added as a tab on the channel.


      9. Alternatively, you may click the Plus icon as seen in the image below to add the application in another way. There will be a search bar where you can type Employee Directory 365 for searching it. Select the Employee Directory 365 application.



      10. Teams Personal App: Click on the three dots shown in the left pane. From here you can also add Employee Directory 365 as a Teams Personal App.



  • Teams app permission policies


      1. Sign in to Teams admin center with the below URL.

        https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/


      2. Under Teams apps select the Permission policies.


      3. From the list of policies click on the Global policy to open it.


      4. Block all apps is the default choice in the dropdown menu for custom apps.


      5. Choose Allow specific apps and block all others from the dropdown menu by clicking it.


      6. By selecting the Allow apps button, you may add other apps.


      7. Search for apps you want to add.


      8. Click on the Add button.


      9. Now click on the Allow button.


      10. Choose that application, then click the Save button.


      11. When the pop-up appears, click on the Confirm button.
