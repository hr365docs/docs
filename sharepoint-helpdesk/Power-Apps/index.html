• How to configure Helpdesk 365 Power Apps?


    Click on the 9 dots icon From the menu, select Power Apps.


    Click on the Solutions option.


    Then, go to the search box, search for your solution, and open it.


    Then, on the left-side search box, search for your environment variable and open it.


    Then, in the right-side search box, search for the display name open it.


    In the current field and default value, please select the site collection where you have deployed Helpdesk 365.


    If you have added a new URL, you will see the same popup as shown in the image below. You will need to click on 'Update Site'.



    After that, your Helpdesk 365 URL will be updated in the field. You just need to click on the 'Save' button to save the changes.


    After saving, go back to Power Apps, select apps, and then Helpdesk 365 will appear.
