    Knowledgebase

    In the Settings section, navigate to Features and enable the Knowledgebase toggle. Once enabled, the Knowledgebase icon will appear in the side navigation bar for quick access.



    You can also enable the Knowledgebase in User’s View option to make published articles visible to end users.



    Under the permissions panel, you can assign which user roles are allowed to create/edit Knowledgebase articles. Only selected roles will be allowed to submit new articles.


    If you need to include approvers outside the predefined roles, you can add them as Additional Approvers by entering their name and email address in the provided field. These users will also receive article approval requests.


    Click the bulb icon in the side navigation bar to open the Knowledgebase.

    To create a new article, click the plus icon (+) at the top right corner of the Knowledgebase page.


    Fill in the required details such as Title, Knowledgebase Type, Team, Service, Questions, Answers, Tags, and Attachments.


    Once all the required information is entered, click the Submit button to save the article.


    If the approval process is enabled, the article will remain pending until approved by an authorized user.


    Articles awaiting approval will display the Approve and Reject buttons at the top of the article page. Approvers can review the content and decide whether to publish or reject it.


    Users can rate published articles using the star rating system and provide written feedback. This helps improve the content quality and relevance for future readers.


    Users with the necessary permissions can perform various actions on a published Knowledgebase article. These actions include editing the article, copying the article link, downloading the article in PDF or Excel format, and deleting the article if required.


    The Knowledgebase also includes options to:

    • Download the complete set of articles in PDF format
    • Delete categories or articles (Admin only)
