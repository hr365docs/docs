      • Feedback

      Feedback: Please click on the help icon and on the Feedback option


      The Rate Your Overall Experience with Timesheet 365 feature allows users to provide feedback on their experience with the application. This feedback helps improve the application by identifying strengths and areas for enhancement, ensuring that user needs are met effectively.



      Product Activation

      Product Activation: Please click on the help icon and on the product activation option, put the key in, and click on validate button.


      Once you click on validate button, the “license validated successfully” message will be displayed then wait for 10 seconds and finally refresh the page.



      After refreshing the page, you can check and verify your license from the help page.
