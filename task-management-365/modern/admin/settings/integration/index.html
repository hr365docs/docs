    Integrations

    This section allows administrators to enable integrations with various 365 applications.



    Employee Onboarding 365 Integration: Enable this toggle to sync onboarding approvals from Employee Onboarding 365. Once enabled, enter your tenant URL to activate the connection.



    Asset Management 365 Integration: Enable this toggle to track and manage IT or physical assets through Asset Management 365. Enter the tenant URL to initiate integration.



    Helpdesk 365 Integration: Enable this toggle to fetch ticketing data and support workflows from Helpdesk 365. Input your tenant URL to complete the setup.



    Time-off Manager 365 Integration: Toggle on this option to synchronize time-off requests and approvals from Time-off Manager 365. Provide your tenant URL to proceed.



    Timesheet 365 Integration: Enable this toggle to display all pending timesheet approvals from Timesheet 365. Enter your tenant URL to complete the connection.



    Expense Tracker 365 Integration: Toggle this on to view all pending approvals from the Expense Tracker 365 system. You will need to enter the tenant URL to enable access.



    Recruitment Management 365 Integration: Enable this option to connect hiring workflows and pending approvals from Recruitment Management 365. Enter the tenant URL after enabling.



    Performance Management 365 Integration: Turn on this toggle to bring performance reviews and approvals from Performance Management 365. Requires tenant URL input.



    CLM365 – Contract Management: When enabled, this integration pulls in contract workflows and approvals. Enter your tenant URL to establish the link.



    Microsoft Planner: Toggle this on to integrate tasks and planning boards from Microsoft Planner into the system. A tenant URL must be provided upon enabling.



    Azure Devops Integration: Use this toggle to sync development pipelines and work items from Azure DevOps. Once enabled, enter your tenant URL for configuration.
