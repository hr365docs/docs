    Advance Settings

      The Advanced Settings section allows administrators to customize key functionalities, including KRA module configurations, KPI settings, and appraisal review delegation. Here, you can streamline performance management processes, ensuring they align with organizational goals and enhance operational efficiency.


      The KRA Module Section allows administrators to customize key components of the self-review process. Here, you can toggle options for features such as Achievements, Self Reflections, and Employee's Action Plan. Additionally, you can manage summaries by both employees and appraisers, agree upon goals, and enforce mandatory KRAs, ensuring a tailored review experience that aligns with organizational objectives.


      Define the approval authority for KPIs by selecting from HR, Manager, or Both. This feature streamlines the approval process, ensuring clarity and accountability in KPI management.


      When enabled, this setting allows managers to directly fill in the employee's self-review, streamlining the appraisal process and enhancing collaboration between managers and their team members.


      When this setting is enabled, it grants higher-level managers the ability to edit the reviews submitted by their direct reports, allowing for comprehensive oversight and ensuring that the self-reviews align with organizational goals.


      The Enable Weightage and Category feature allows users to assign weightage to different Key Result Areas (KRAs) and categorize them for better performance assessment. Users can toggle these settings on or off to customize how performance is evaluated, ensuring that both target and achieved metrics are clearly defined and measurable.


      The Column Settings feature provides users with the ability to customize the visibility and arrangement of key review components. With three options available Self Review, Appraiser's Review, and HR Review users can easily configure and change these settings.


      The Self Review Column feature allows users to easily drag and drop elements for customized review organization. This intuitive setup ensures a streamlined and personalized self-review experience.


      The Appraiser's Review feature enables users to drag and drop elements for tailored organization of appraisal items. This functionality enhances the review process, allowing for a streamlined and personalized experience for appraisers.


      The HR Review Column feature allows users to drag and drop elements for customized organization of HR appraisal items. This functionality streamlines the HR review process, ensuring a tailored and efficient experience.


      In this system, Question Weightage calculates ratings based on individual question weights, while Category Weightage calculates ratings based on the overall weight of each category, ensuring balanced evaluations.


      Select your preferred option from the list, and your rating will be assigned based on your choice.
