    Notification


      Notification settings or email templates users could change the body of the email (changing the text in the email body or adding placeholders).

      The notification has Six categories: Self Review, appraisers review, HR review, 360 Degree, 1:1, and KPI.


      Self-Review

      By clicking the edit icon in the Self Review notifications, you can easily customize and modify the email notifications. This allows you to tailor the communication to suit your needs and ensure clarity for recipients.



    • It triggers notifications to the appraisee and has different notification templates.


    • Review Announcement: A reminder notification could be triggered to all the appraisees. (One day before the self-review could start)


    • Review Notifications: Notifications are triggered to all appraisees on the self-review start date.


    • Last Day Reminder: Reminder notifications are triggered to all the appraisees a day before the self-review time deadline.


    • Last Day Notification: Notifications are triggered to all the appraisees on the last day of the self-review timeline.

    • Review Completion Notification: Notifications are triggered to all the appraisees on the Review Completion


    • Review Resubmit: Notifications are triggered to all the appraisees to review again and resubmit .


      • Appraisal’s-Review

    • By clicking the edit icon in the Appraiser Review notifications, you can easily customize and modify the email notifications. This allows you to tailor the communication to suit your needs and ensure clarity for recipients.



      Notifications are triggers to the appraisers (Manger of appraisee) and have different notifications templates.


    • Review Announcement: Notifications are triggered or sent to all appraisers one day in advance of the appraiser’s start date or on a self-review timeline. (Appraiser review starts next day of self-review timeline)


    • Review Notifications: Notifications are triggered or sent to all appraisers on the appraisal-review set start date. i.e., on the next day of the self-review timeline.


    • Last Day Reminder: The notifications are triggered or sent to all appraisers on a day before the appraisal review timeline.


    • Last Day Notification: Notifications are triggered to all the appraisers on the day of the appraisal-review timeline.


    • Additional appraiser's review completion: Notification are triggered on the Completion of Additional Review.


    • Additional Appraiser Request: Notification are triggered to Manager for Additional Appraiser Request.


    • Release Review: Notification are triggered when your review is released.


      • HR-Review

      By clicking the edit icon in the HR365 Review notifications, you can easily customize and modify the email notifications. This allows you to tailor the communication to suit your needs and ensure clarity for recipients.



      Notifications are triggered or sent to the HR executives. In this category, they are different notifications


    • Review Announcement: Notifications are triggered or sent to HR executives (Team) before one day of the HR review start date or on an appraiser review timeline.


    • Review Notifications: Notifications are triggered or sent to HR executives (Team)on the HR review start date. i.e., on the next day of the appraiser-review timeline.


    • Last Day Reminder: Notifications are triggered or sent to HR executives (Team) on a day before the HR review time deadline.


    • Last Day Notification: Notifications are triggered or sent to HR executives (Team) on the day of the HR executive review timeline.


    • Request To Extend Review (Request): Notification is triggered to request extend the number of days for self-review.


    • Request To Extend Review (Approved): Notification is triggered to Approved extend the number of days for self-review.


    • Request To Extend Review (Rejected): Notification is triggered to Rejected extend the number of days for self-review.


      • All reminder's notifications work with the help of power automation.

      Self-review start date and timeline are defined during appraisal cycle creation.

      Appraisal review starts on the next day of the self-review timeline, the appraiser review timeline is defined during appraisal cycle creation.

      HR review starts on the next day of the appraiser-review timeline, the HR review timeline is defined during appraisal cycle creation.

      KPI

      By clicking the edit icon in the KPI Review notifications, you can easily customize and modify the email notifications. This allows you to tailor the communication to suit your needs and ensure clarity for recipients.




    • Task Notification: Notification is triggered when task Assign.
