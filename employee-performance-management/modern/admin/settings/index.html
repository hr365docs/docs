    Advance Settings


      The Advanced Settings section allows administrators to customize key functionalities, including KRA module configurations, KPI settings, and appraisal review delegation. Here, you can streamline performance management processes, ensuring they align with organizational goals and enhance operational efficiency.


      The KRA Module Section allows administrators to customize key components of the self-review process. Here, you can toggle options for features such as Achievements, Self Reflections, and Employee's Action Plan. Additionally, you can manage summaries by both employees and appraisers, agree upon goals, and enforce mandatory KRAs, ensuring a tailored review experience that aligns with organizational objectives.


      Define the approval authority for KPIs by selecting from HR, Manager, or Both. This feature streamlines the approval process, ensuring clarity and accountability in KPI management.


      When enabled, this setting allows managers to directly fill in the employee's self-review, streamlining the appraisal process and enhancing collaboration between managers and their team members.


      When this setting is enabled, it grants higher-level managers the ability to edit the reviews submitted by their direct reports, allowing for comprehensive oversight and ensuring that the self-reviews align with organizational goals.


      The Enable Weightage and Category feature allows users to assign weightage to different Key Result Areas (KRAs) and categorize them for better performance assessment. Users can toggle these settings on or off to customize how performance is evaluated, ensuring that both target and achieved metrics are clearly defined and measurable.


      The Column Settings feature provides users with the ability to customize the visibility and arrangement of key review components. With three options available Self Review, Appraiser's Review, and HR Review users can easily configure and change these settings.


      The Self Review Column feature allows users to easily drag and drop elements for customized review organization. This intuitive setup ensures a streamlined and personalized self-review experience.


      The Appraiser's Review feature enables users to drag and drop elements for tailored organization of appraisal items. This functionality enhances the review process, allowing for a streamlined and personalized experience for appraisers.


      The HR Review Column feature allows users to drag and drop elements for customized organization of HR appraisal items. This functionality streamlines the HR review process, ensuring a tailored and efficient experience.


      In this system, Question Weightage calculates ratings based on individual question weights, while Category Weightage calculates ratings based on the overall weight of each category, ensuring balanced evaluations.


      Select your preferred option from the list, and your rating will be assigned based on your choice.


    Notification


      Notification settings or email templates users could change the body of the email (changing the text in the email body or adding placeholders).

      The notification has Six categories: Self Review, appraisers review, HR review, 360 Degree, 1:1, and KPI.


      Self-Review

      By clicking the edit icon in the Self Review notifications, you can easily customize and modify the email notifications. This allows you to tailor the communication to suit your needs and ensure clarity for recipients.



    • It triggers notifications to the appraisee and has different notification templates.


    • Review Announcement: A reminder notification could be triggered to all the appraisees. (One day before the self-review could start)


    • Review Notifications: Notifications are triggered to all appraisees on the self-review start date.


    • Last Day Reminder: Reminder notifications are triggered to all the appraisees a day before the self-review time deadline.


    • Last Day Notification: Notifications are triggered to all the appraisees on the last day of the self-review timeline.

    • Review Completion Notification: Notifications are triggered to all the appraisees on the Review Completion


    • Review Resubmit: Notifications are triggered to all the appraisees to review again and resubmit .


      • Appraisal’s-Review

    • By clicking the edit icon in the Appraiser Review notifications, you can easily customize and modify the email notifications. This allows you to tailor the communication to suit your needs and ensure clarity for recipients.



      Notifications are triggers to the appraisers (Manger of appraisee) and have different notifications templates.


    • Review Announcement: Notifications are triggered or sent to all appraisers one day in advance of the appraiser’s start date or on a self-review timeline. (Appraiser review starts next day of self-review timeline)


    • Review Notifications: Notifications are triggered or sent to all appraisers on the appraisal-review set start date. i.e., on the next day of the self-review timeline.


    • Last Day Reminder: The notifications are triggered or sent to all appraisers on a day before the appraisal review timeline.


    • Last Day Notification: Notifications are triggered to all the appraisers on the day of the appraisal-review timeline.


    • Additional appraiser's review completion: Notification are triggered on the Completion of Additional Review.


    • Additional Appraiser Request: Notification are triggered to Manager for Additional Appraiser Request.


    • Release Review: Notification are triggered when your review is released.


      • HR-Review

      By clicking the edit icon in the HR365 Review notifications, you can easily customize and modify the email notifications. This allows you to tailor the communication to suit your needs and ensure clarity for recipients.



      Notifications are triggered or sent to the HR executives. In this category, they are different notifications


    • Review Announcement: Notifications are triggered or sent to HR executives (Team) before one day of the HR review start date or on an appraiser review timeline.


    • Review Notifications: Notifications are triggered or sent to HR executives (Team)on the HR review start date. i.e., on the next day of the appraiser-review timeline.


    • Last Day Reminder: Notifications are triggered or sent to HR executives (Team) on a day before the HR review time deadline.


    • Last Day Notification: Notifications are triggered or sent to HR executives (Team) on the day of the HR executive review timeline.


    • Request To Extend Review (Request): Notification is triggered to request extend the number of days for self-review.


    • Request To Extend Review (Approved): Notification is triggered to Approved extend the number of days for self-review.


    • Request To Extend Review (Rejected): Notification is triggered to Rejected extend the number of days for self-review.


      • All reminder's notifications work with the help of power automation.

      Self-review start date and timeline are defined during appraisal cycle creation.

      Appraisal review starts on the next day of the self-review timeline, the appraiser review timeline is defined during appraisal cycle creation.

      HR review starts on the next day of the appraiser-review timeline, the HR review timeline is defined during appraisal cycle creation.

    Features


    • Ratings: Different rating scales could be mapped or selected for the appraisal cycle (Appraisal cycle creation). The rating scale plays a role during self-review (appraisee review), appraiser review, and HR review of the appraisal cycle.


      Click on + Add to Add Rating and select the Rating Scale.




    • 360 Feedback : Different rating scales are added while mapping or selecting for appraisal cycle in appraisal cycle creation. The rating scale plays a role in 360 feedback ratings. Appraisee can request and receive feedback during their appraisal cycle.




    • Rating Verbatim: Shown according to the users Performance.



    • Appraisal Cycle: Appraisal cycle could be created, and users could be added.

      All the required details could be added in the appraisal cycle as mentioned below and could be saved by clicking the save button.


      1. Name of the Appraisal Cycle: Please enter the name of the appraisal cycle to distinguish based on the relevance you may have.


      2. Select Appraisal Cycle Duration: The duration of the appraisal cycle can be selected based on your organization’s review cycle duration.


      3. Appraisal Cycle Start: The start date of the appraisal cycle could be allocated for an employee to be reviewed, in the above case it is for an annual review cycle, Example: The review start date is July 1st, 2022.


      4. Appraisal Cycle End: The end date of the appraisal cycle could be allocated for an employee to be reviewed, in the above case it is for an annual review cycle, Example: The review start date is July 1st, 2022, and the review end date (considering Annual review cycle) it would be on June 30th, 2025.


      5. Review start date: Define the date for which the app will be open to doing the self-reviews/ appraiser’s reviews in this app.


      6. No of days for Self-Review: Define the number of days allowed to perform self-review by employees. Note: Employees should complete their self-review before it moves to the next stage of the appraiser’s review.


      7. No of days for Appraiser's Review: Define the number of days allowed for appraisers to review employees. Appraisers must and should complete their activity before it moves to the next stage of collaboration with the HR/leadership team.


      8. No of days for Review Conclusion: Define the number of days allowed to conclude the review cycle with internal HR/leadership/payroll / etc. meetings.


      9. Enable Feedback: 360 feedback request option is shown to the appraisee.


      10. Rating Scale:Users could self-review or self-rate themselves (appraisee review), appraiser review, and HR review of the appraisal cycle.

        11. KPI Cycle:Streamlining Performance Evaluation with the KPI Cycle

        The KPI Cycle in our application allows users to add additional performance points. Once submitted, managers review, approve, or reject the KPIs, ensuring accountability. Approved KPIs seamlessly integrate into the user's self-review, fostering continuous growth and enhancing performance visibility.


        The KPI Cycle creation form enables users to set up and manage key performance indicators. Users can define the cycle's name, start and end dates, and duration while specifying the timeframe for adding and finalizing KPIs. This streamlined process ensures clear deadlines and accountability, enhancing performance evaluations and aligning contributions with organizational objectives.


        After creating the KPI cycle, the admin can easily add appraisees by clicking the "Manage Appraisee" icon, which takes them to the employee selection page. Here, users can be selected for evaluation. Once selections are made, clicking the save button finalizes the additions, ensuring an efficient and organized performance management process.



        To remove users from the KPI cycle, click on the delete icon. To add users, simply click the add button. This functionality allows for easy management of participants in the KPI evaluation process.


        The 1:1 meeting feature allows employees to schedule focused discussions with colleagues. These sessions facilitate the exchange of updates and address challenges while outlining key action and talking points. By promoting open communication, they enhance collaboration and strengthen team dynamics. Ultimately, 1:1 meetings drive positive outcomes in the workplace.


        In the module settings, users can manage the visibility of the KPI or self-review settings. They have the option to enable or disable these modules by toggling them on or off. This allows for customization of which features are displayed to users within the application.


        he user can choose to display information according to their preferences, including the Job Title, which specifies the role held; the Department, indicating the specific area of work; the Company, which represents the organization; and the Location, detailing where the company is based.


        In the Self Review section, a PDF guide is attached to assist you in filling out your review. Please download the PDF, follow the instructions step by step, and ensure all required fields are completed accurately before submitting.
