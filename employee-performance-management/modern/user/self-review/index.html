    Self Review

    In self-review, the user can give a rating to himself, according to his performance, and can review his rating.

    1. Review
    2. Summary and Plan

    Review

    Inside the review, we have some different categories and inside some KRA(Key Responsibility Area) there are questions that are created by the admin for the user. In which the user can give himself a rating according to his performance and finally in the overall rating, the user can check his overall rating.



    Here Deliverables, Customer Focus, Operational, and People are different type of categories which is created by the admin for the user. There are some questions inside KRA(Key Responsibility Area) that are created by the admin. user can give only a rating to himself through these questions and can also add self-Rating remarks.




    In the above image Overall Rating, the user can check the overall self Rating in the form of Simple Rating, Weighted Rating, and Absolute Rating.

    Summary and Plan

    Summary and plan are like feedback in which the user gives his feedback. Here the user gives feedback on his performance.



    1. Achievements:  Here the user writes about what he/she did last year and what achievements he achieved. After that click on Save and Next button and move to the next tab.



    2. Self Reflections:  Here the user can write about their positive attributes and gives a written view of their performance cycle it involves their ratings established goals, competencies, and overall performance.



    3. Employee's Action Plan:  Here users can write about plans, which means what he/she will do for future tasks. which he/she will achieve the goal in the future. After that click on Save and Next button and move to the next tab.



    4. Summary By Employee:  Here user can write about his overall performance, which means what he/she did in the past year and what he/she will do in the future, what he/she achieved in past, and what he will achieve in the future.



      5. After completing the summary and plan select the checkbox and sign it by typing your name then click on the Sign button same as the below image.



      After clicking on the Sign button, your self-review is successfully submitted shown in the below image.
