Dashboard

The Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of email productivity metrics within Microsoft 365. It helps users analyze their communication trends, monitor performance, and gain insights into sent, received, and opened emails.



Sent Emails, Recipients, Received Emails, and Senders

These key statistics provide an overview of total Sent Emails, number of Recipients reached, Received Emails count, and the unique Senders engaged. This helps users quickly evaluate their overall communication volume and reach within a selected time period.


Emails Sent by Day

The Emails Sent by Day chart visualizes the daily distribution of outgoing emails. It allows users to track communication patterns, identify high-activity days, and adjust their outreach strategies accordingly.


Time Until First Open

This chart displays how long it takes recipients to open an email after it has been sent. It’s segmented into time intervals (e.g., <15m, 1h, 6h, 1d, etc.), helping users measure responsiveness and optimize send times for better engagement.


Opening Rate

The Opening Rate donut chart indicates the percentage of emails opened compared to the total sent. This provides valuable insight into email engagement and helps users assess the effectiveness of their communication campaigns.


Filter Options

Users can apply filters such as My Emails or Team Emails and adjust the time range (e.g., This Month) to refine the dashboard view. These filters allow users to analyze personal performance or team-wide email productivity effectively.
