Introduction

Asset management App, Microsoft asset management is fixed asset management & Azure asset management software that helps in finding location, usage, cost, life, and renewals of services in a couple of clicks and automates your asset’s lifecycles. This fixed asset management software has a barcode asset management system and you can track the cost, location, status, inventory, expiry, or renewal of physical and IT assets, throughout their lifecycle. Once an asset is assigned or deployed, this asset management software records all activities and provides you regular updates and reports up to the life cycle once the asset is retired. But a user can see the asset details assigned by the admin.
