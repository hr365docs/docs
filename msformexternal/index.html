• External users (Non-MS Users) MS Form Integration with Helpdesk365


      You can follow these steps in case the HR365 Helpdesk Modern solution has been already configured.

    1. Please go to the settings and click on ‘Integrate Microsoft Form with Helpdesk 365’.
    2. This will opens Integration page, now enable the “Integrate Microsoft Form with Helpdesk”.
    3. Copy the URL of the ‘HR365 Helpdesk, and open it in a new tab.
    4. Duplicate the form by Clicking on the ‘Duplicate it’ button.
    5. Once you duplicate it, you can rename the form as per your choice by clicking on the form name. Note: This MS Form is accessible to MS Users and Non-MS users.
    6. Click on the collect responses button, this opens the popup. Copy the click by clicking on the copy link button.
    7. Copy the Form ID from the URL as shown below. Here you need to copy complete id fond next to id= in the URL .
    8. Now go to the power automate solution through the below link and open the HR365 Helpdesk Modern.
      9. https://make.powerautomate.com/
    9. Now go to the ‘cloud flows’ and turn on the ‘MS Ticket Form’.
    10. Now you can test it. Please open the Duplicated MS form, fill in the details, and submit it.
    11. Then check that request was submitted through the MS form creating a ticket helpdesk 365 home page.
