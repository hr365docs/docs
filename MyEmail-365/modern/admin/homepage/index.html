  • Home Page

      • The My Email 365 Home Page provides a simple and intuitive card that displays the number of unread emails from your Outlook inbox. It helps users stay updated with their messages and access Outlook quickly, all from within SharePoint.


    The Home Page shows the My Email 365 card with key features:

    • Card Title: Displays “My Email” by default.


    • Unread Count: Shows the number of unread emails.


    • Open Outlook Button: A quick-access button to open Outlook Web or Outlook Desktop.



    • Shrink Icon: Reduces the size of the card, giving you more screen space.


    • Expand Icon: Enlarges the card to provide a detailed view with more visibility.


    Editing Card Details: Click the pencil icon on the card to open the configuration panel.


    You can change the card title, customize the button label, select the default Outlook experience (Web or Desktop), and save your changes.


    Settings: Access the global settings to adjust theme and manage users. Choose from Light, Dark, or Site theme, and configure user permissions (Admin/User roles, add or remove users).


    Help: The Help panel provides license details, version info, subscription validity, and links to Support, FAQ, Privacy Policy, Release Notes, Product Activation, and License Validation.
