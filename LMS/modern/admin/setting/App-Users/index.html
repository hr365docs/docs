App Users

This section allows you to Create users according to your preferences.


  • Click on Configure to process Further.


  • A new screen will be displayed.


  • Click on + Add Button to create new Section.


  • Complete all the fields in the 'Add User' , ensuring that all necessary details are filled out accurately. Once done, click 'Save' to store the information.


  • You can toggle the switch to "Off," and the active users will then appear in the inactive users tab.


  • In this Inactive Users tab, only users who are currently inactive will be displayed.
