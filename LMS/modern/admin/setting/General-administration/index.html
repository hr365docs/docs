General Administration

The General Administration section allows administrators to configure core learning attributes such as course levels, types, categories, required skills, and job titles. These settings help standardize and organize learning content, ensuring consistent classification and easier management across the LMS.


Course Level:

Course Level defines the difficulty or stage of a course, such as Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced. You can add the desired type of course level you want to categorize and organize courses effectively.


Types:

Types in LMS 365 define the format or nature of a course, such as online, classroom, or blended learning. You can add the desired type to organize courses and align them with different learning delivery methods.


Categories:

Categories in LMS 365 help group courses based on departments, topics, or business areas for easy navigation. You can create and manage categories to organize learning content and make courses easier to find.


Required Skills:

List the technical, professional, and soft skills needed to perform this role effectively. These skills help ensure successful task execution and alignment with job responsibilities.
