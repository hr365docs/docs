Home Page

    The LMS 365 home page provides a centralized and user-friendly learning dashboard that helps users manage their entire learning experience efficiently. It includes key sections such as Course Catalog, My Courses, My Assessments, Completed, My Team, and My Training, enabling learners to easily browse available courses, track assigned and completed training, view assessments, and monitor team progress. With built-in filters, ratings, and course details, users can quickly discover new learning opportunities, manage their learning activities, and stay on top of their training requirements.



    Course Catalog:

    The Course Catalog provides a centralized view of all available training courses within the LMS. Users can easily browse, search, and filter courses to find relevant learning opportunities based on their interests and roles.



    My Courses:

    My Courses displays all courses assigned or enrolled to the user in one place. It allows learners to easily track progress, access course content, and continue their training at their own pace.



    My Assessments:

    My Assessments shows all assessments assigned to you, including quizzes, tests, and evaluations. It helps you track assessment status, attempt deadlines, and review results in one place.



    Here’s Completed tab appears below Assessments, showing all the tasks you have successfully finished.



    Completed:

    The Completed tab displays all courses and learning activities you have successfully finished. It allows you to review course details, completion dates, and performance ratings in one place. Use this section to track your learning progress and revisit completed training when needed.



    My Team:

    The My Team tab provides an overview of training assigned to your team members and their progress status. It helps you track completion, identify learners who are in progress or not started, and manage team learning effectively.



    My Training:

    The My Training tab displays all training programs assigned to you, along with their duration, course count, and progress details. You can group and manage multiple courses under a single training, making it easier to track and complete related learning in one place.
