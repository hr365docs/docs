Home Page

    The homepage of the HR365 Learning Management System provides a comprehensive dashboard, offering features such as Assessments, Available Courses, Enrolled Courses, Favorites, and Completed Courses. These features help users seamlessly manage their learning journey, explore new opportunities, track progress, and revisit completed content.


    Assessments

    The Assessments section includes two tabs: Assigned and Completed. The Assigned tab displays tasks that are currently pending, while the Completed tab lists assessments that have already been finished.



    The Assigned tab displays all learning tasks that have been assigned to you.



    The Completed tab shows the assessments you have successfully completed.



    Available Learnings

    The Available Learnings section lists all the learning modules currently accessible to you.



    Clicking on View Learning opens a detailed course view, including the description, content outline, assessments, and other relevant information.



    In Progress

    This section displays the courses you are currently working on, allowing easy access to continue your learning.



    Upcoming

    This section displays the courses that are scheduled for the future, helping you plan your learning journey.



    Completed Learnings

    The Completed Learnings section displays all courses and trainings you have successfully finished.



My Favourites

The My Favourites tab shows courses that you have marked as favorites for quick reference and easy access later.



My Team

This section allows you to view the learning progress of your team members, helping you track and support their development journey.





Settings

This section allows you to customize your homepage view based on your preferences.

  1. Click on the Settings icon to choose the sections you want to display on your homepage.



  2. Select the desired sections using the checkboxes, then click Save to apply your changes.
