    Dashboard


    CLM365 provides two primary dashboards designed to streamline contract and request visibility across the platform:

    • Contract Dashboard
    • Request Dashboard


      Contract

    Contract Dashboard: Provides a centralized view of all contracts and contract requests, enabling efficient monitoring, management, and tracking throughout the contract lifecycle.

    Contracts by Type

    Shows the distribution of contracts across different categories, providing a clear understanding of the types of agreements managed within the system.


    Contracts by Status

    Displays contracts according to their current status, helping track workflow stages and progress across all agreements.


    Contracts by Assignee

    Highlights the number of contracts assigned to each individual, allowing visibility into workloads and responsibilities.


    Contracts Over Time

    Tracks contract activity over a period of time, showing trends and identifying periods of high or low activity.


    Expiring Contracts

    Provides an overview of contracts approaching their end dates, supporting proactive renewal or termination planning.


    Top Contracts by Type

    Highlights the most common contract categories, offering insight into frequently used agreements within the organization.


    Top Contracts by Counterparty

    Shows the counterparties most frequently involved in contracts, providing visibility into key relationships and partnerships.


    Contract Costs

    Displays the cost distribution across contracts over time, allowing monitoring of financial trends and budget allocation.


    Settings

    Use the settings options to adjust the layout, reorganize sections, or modify which elements are displayed.

    Charts can be customized, moved, or removed to enhance visualization and improve dashboard clarity.


      Request

    Request Dashboard: Provides a complete view of all contract requests, allowing visibility into each stage from initiation to final approval and publication for efficient tracking and management.


    Requests by Type

    Displays the distribution of requests across different categories, providing insight into the types of requests being processed.


    Requests by Status

    Shows requests according to their current status, helping monitor progress and identify bottlenecks in the approval process.


    Requests by Assignee

    Highlights requests assigned to individuals, providing visibility into workloads and responsibility allocation.


    Requests Over Time

    Tracks the volume of requests over a period of time, showing trends and periods of increased activity.


    Request Aging

    Illustrates the aging of requests, helping identify requests that require attention based on how long they have been pending.


    Top Requests by Type

    Highlights the most frequently submitted request types, providing insight into common organizational needs.


    Top Requests by Counterparty

    Displays the counterparties most frequently involved in requests, offering insight into key relationships.


    Settings

    Use the settings options to adjust the dashboard layout, reorganize sections, or modify which elements are displayed.

    Requests can be customized, moved, or removed to improve visualization and enhance clarity.
