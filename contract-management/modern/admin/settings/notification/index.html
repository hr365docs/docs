      Notifications

      Access and manage email templates and tracking under the Notifications section.


      Email Templates List: View, filter, and manage contract-related email templates including expiry, termination, and renewal.


      Add/Edit Email Template: Configure custom email notifications with multiple reminder options and targeted recipients.

      Task Templates Overview: View and manage automated task notifications such as assignments, reminders, and escalations.


      Add/Edit Task Template: Customize task-based alerts with configurable timing and designated recipients for effective tracking and follow-up.

      Obligation Templates Overview: Manage and configure notifications related to obligation assignments and task reminders.


      Add/Edit Obligation Template: Define obligation-based alerts, set notification intervals, and select recipient roles for better compliance tracking.

    Email Tracker


    • The Email Tracker module provides a centralized view of task reminder emails, allowing administrators to quickly assess delivery and user engagement. It supports effective tracking and accountability by highlighting who received, opened, and interacted with notifications.




      The Email Tracker includes filter options that allow users to refine results based on the current month, year, or through a free-text search. This makes it easier to locate specific emails and monitor recent activity efficiently.



      Detailed Interaction Logs: By selecting a recipient’s name from the summary, administrators can view a detailed interaction log. This log includes each open event with supporting metadata such as date and time, IP address, geographical location, browser type, operating system, and device used.




      This feature ensures transparency around system-generated communications and provides valuable insights for operational follow-ups, compliance checks, and user behavior analysis.
