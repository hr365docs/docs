    Notifications


      In the Notifications section, each action triggers a distinct email. You have the flexibility to customize the email template, enable or disable it, and add CC recipients according to your preferences.


      Email Templates

      The application allows you to customize email templates and add placeholders based on the template type. These templates are triggered automatically according to their purpose and will be reflected in the emails sent to users.


      Template Types

      In the Email Template section, different template types are available.


      Using Placeholders
      • Click on the Placeholder option to view nine types of placeholders.




      • Placeholders can be inserted wherever necessary within the email template.
      • Once added, the placeholder values will be dynamically populated and displayed directly in the email.


      This flexibility ensures that email communication remains accurate, consistent, and tailored to each scenario.
