Dashboard

This dashboard serves as a centralized hub for monitoring and analyzing organizational expenses in real-time.



Expenses by Type

Illustrates the distribution of expenses across categories such as Accommodation, Travel, Food, and Entertainment. Helps identify dominant expense categories, allowing for targeted cost control and budget optimization.

Expenses by Department

Showcases how expenses are allocated across departments including Engineering, Marketing, HR, and Sales. Facilitates inter-departmental budget analysis, aiding in resource distribution and performance assessment.


Expenses by Project

Breaks down expenses by specific projects, supporting precise tracking of project costs and ensuring efficient resource allocation and financial oversight.


Expenses by Status

Illustrates the status of expenses, categorized as Approved, Pending, Rejected, or To Be Approved. Enhances transparency in the approval process, helping to address bottlenecks and streamline financial operations.


Approval Status

Focuses on the approval pipeline, providing a snapshot of approval progress and enabling timely interventions to maintain workflow efficiency.


Expenses Created This Month

Tracks daily expense creation within the current month. Supports short-term financial planning by identifying daily spending patterns and potential anomalies.


Expenses Created This Year

Offers a monthly overview of expenses for the current year. Aids in long-term budgeting and forecasting by revealing yearly expenditure trends.


Monthly Expenses by Employee & Location

Analyzes monthly expenses by employee and location. Enables localized cost control and accountability, ensuring accurate expense attribution.
