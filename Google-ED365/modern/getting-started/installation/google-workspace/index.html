    Installation of Employee Directory 365 on Google Workspace

    Prerequisites:

    1. Only Google Workspace Admins can install this application for the domain.
    2. Make sure required permissions are granted to allow access to user directory and data APIs.


  1. Visit Google Workspace Marketplace and search for Employee Directory 365.


  2. Click on the app from the search results, then click Admin install.


  3. Click on the Continue button to proceed.


  4. Accept the Terms and Conditions by selecting the checkbox and click Finish button.


  5. Click Done to complete the setup process.


  6. Open the Google Apps Launcher and locate the Employee Directory 365 application. Click to launch it.


  7. Sign in using your Google Workspace account credentials.


  8. When the Google sign-in window appears, select your Google Workspace account from the list. If it's not listed, click Use another account, enter your email address manually.


  9. Enter your password and click Next.


  10. Click on Continue to proceed to grant the necessary permissions.


  11. Select the permissions you want to grant Employee Directory 365, or allow all recommended access, and click Continue.


  12. After successful authentication, you will be redirected to the Google Employee Directory 365 homepage.


Once installation and authentication are complete, the Employee Directory 365 application will be available to use from your Google Apps menu. It integrates with Google Workspace to manage and display employee profiles securely.
