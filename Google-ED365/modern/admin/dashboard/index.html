Dashboard

The dashboard provides a centralized and user-friendly interface offering a visual overview of key information and functionalities across the organization.

1. Organization

This section highlights workforce-related metrics and organizational structure, providing insights into employee distribution, tenure, diversity, and more.



  • Total user headcount.
  • Count of Full-Time employees.
  • Average gender distribution by year.
  • Average user age (in years).
  • Average tenure from DOJ to current date.

New Employees by Month

This graph showcases monthly new employee registrations within the current year.


Total Employees by Department

Displays distribution of employees across departments. Users without a department are excluded.


Employment Type

Shows counts of employees by employment classification such as Full Time or Part Time.


Average Age by Department

Displays average age of employees in each department.


Average Age by Location

Displays average age of employees by their work location.


Average Tenure by Department

Shows average tenure (in years) of employees by department.


Gender Diversity by Department

Presents gender diversity as percentages across different departments.


Gender Diversity by Location

Presents gender diversity by location, expressed in percentages.


Average Tenure by Location

Shows average employee tenure grouped by location.


2. Analytics

This section focuses on platform engagement and user activity analytics across modules.

  • Module Visits by Users: Monitors how often each user visits different modules.
  • Average Time Spent per Module (Mins): Displays how much time users spend on average per module.
  • Top 10 Active Users: Lists the most engaged users based on activity levels.
  • Module Popularity (%): Visualizes which modules are used most frequently as a percentage of total usage.
