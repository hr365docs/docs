Help

  • Please verify the details provided in the Google Workspace Employee Directory application.
  • For any queries, reach out to HR365 through the Help panel.
  • Your feedback is valuable and helps us improve the application.
  • Use the QR code to share or download employee information.
  • Click on "License Activated" to view the currently active license.
  • Click on "License Validation" to verify and validate your license.
  • Check the current version of the application from the version info section.
  • Visit the FAQs section for quick answers to common questions.


    Product Activation

    Please click on the help icon and on the product activation option, put the key in, and click on validate button.



    Once you click on validate button, the license validated successfully message is displayed then wait for 10 seconds and finally refresh the page.



    After refreshing the page, you can check and verify your license from the help page.
