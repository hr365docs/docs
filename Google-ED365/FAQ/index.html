How to Sign In to Google Employee Directory 365 Using Your Google Workspace Account
  1. Open the Application and Navigate to: https://employee-directory.com
  • Click the "Sign in with Google" button located at the center of the landing page.


  • When the Google sign-in window opens, select your Google Workspace account from the list or enter your credentials.


  • If your account isn't listed, click “Use another account” and enter your email address manually.
    Then, type your password and click “Next” to proceed.


  • After successful authentication, you will be redirected to the Google Employee Directory 365 homepage.
