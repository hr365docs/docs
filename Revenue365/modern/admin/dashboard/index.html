  • Dashboard

    This dashboard provides a real-time overview of key financial and billing metrics, such as revenue growth, invoice status, subscription trends, and client performance. It helps users monitor billing activities, track invoice lifecycles, and make informed business decisions.

    Top Metrics Summary

    • Invoice Created: Total number of invoices generated.
    • Revenue Growth: Revenue generated in the current month.
    • Paid: Number of fully paid invoices.
    • Pending: Invoices awaiting payment.
    • Overdue: Invoices that are past the due date.
    • Approved: Invoices approved for billing.


    Invoice Status

    This donut chart displays the distribution of invoices by their current status: Paid, Overdue, and Sent.


    Revenue Growth

    Line graph showing the revenue generated daily in the current month. Peaks indicate increased invoice or payment activity.


    Subscription Growth

    Tracks subscription activity including Renewals, New Subscriptions, and Cancellations. Helps evaluate recurring revenue flow.


    Revenue by Client

    This chart displays the revenue contribution from each client, helping identify high-value customers.


    Products vs Services

    Compares revenue from products versus services, allowing teams to evaluate performance in each category.


    Products/Subscriptions Sold

    Shows the total number of products and subscriptions sold during the selected time period.
