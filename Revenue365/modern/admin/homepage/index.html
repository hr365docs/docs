  • Home Page



    • Transactions : View and manage financial transactions related to revenue collection and processing.


      • To add a transaction, click on Add Transaction and fill in the transaction details. Finally, click on Paid to complete the transaction.


      When you click on the Invoice ID, you will be redirected to a detailed invoice template page that displays complete invoice informations. At the top right of the page, you will find four options: View Request History, Download as PDF, Edit Invoice, and Share Invoice.



      Proforma Invoices: Draft invoices issued before final billing — useful for pre-approval or estimation purposes. Click on Proforma Invoice to add a new one, fill in the required details, and then click on the Save and Preview button.



      When you click on the Proforma Invoice ID, you will be redirected to a detailed invoice template page that displays all the standard invoice informations. The top section of the page provides options such as View Request History, Download as PDF, Edit Invoice, Share Invoice, and a Convertion option to convert the proforma invoice into a final invoice.



      Quotations: Formal documents provided to clients to outline pricing and terms before confirming a deal — useful for negotiation or approval stages. Click on Quotation to add a new one, fill in the required details, and then click on the Save and Preview button.



      When you click on the Quotation ID, you will be redirected to a detailed quotation template page that displays all standard quotation information. The top section of the page provides options such as View Request History, Download as PDF, Edit Quotation, Share Quotation, and a Conversion option to convert the quotation into either a Proforma Invoice or a Final Invoice.



      Invoices: Final billing documents issued to clients after goods or services are delivered — used for payment collection and record-keeping. Click on Invoice to add a new one, fill in the required details, and then click on the Save and Preview button.



      When you click on the Invoice ID, you will be redirected to a detailed invoice template page that displays all standard invoice information. The top section of the page provides options such as View Request History, Download as PDF, Edit Invoice, and Share Invoice.



    • Products: List of revenue-generating products that can be quoted, invoiced, or subscribed to.


      • Click on the Add button to add a new product. Fill in all the required details, then click on Save and Add More to continue adding products.



      If you click on the Name under the product list, an Edit option will be available. After making changes, click on Update to save the modifications.


    • Services: List of billable services that can be quoted, invoiced, or subscribed to.



      • Click on the Add button to add a new service. Fill in all the required details, then click on Save and Add More to continue adding services.


      If you click on the Name under the service list, an Edit option will be available. After making changes, click on Update to save the modifications.


    • Subscriptions: Recurring service plans that can be quoted, invoiced, and billed periodically based on selected intervals.


      • When you click on the Add button in the Subscriptions section, a form will open where you can enter the subscription details. After entering all the required details, click on Save and Add More to continue adding additional subscriptions.


      If you click on the Subscription ID from the list, an Edit option will be available. Make the necessary updates and then click on Update to save the changes.


    • Bundles: A combination of multiple products or services grouped together and offered as a single package — useful for selling predefined solutions or plans.


      • When you click on the Add button in the Bundles section, a form will open where you can enter the bundle details. After entering all the required information such as Name, Bundle ID, Description, HSN/SAC, and selecting the components in the Bundle field, click on Save and Add More to continue adding additional bundles.


      If you click on the Bundle Name from the list, an Edit option will be available. Make the necessary updates and then click on Update to save the changes.
