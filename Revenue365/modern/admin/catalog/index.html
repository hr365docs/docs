Catalog

When you click on Catalog, you will see the following four options:

  1. Products – Add and manage individual product offerings with associated pricing and details.
  2. Services – Define service-based offerings such as consulting, implementation, or support.
  3. Subscriptions – Set up recurring revenue models by configuring subscription-based items.
  4. Bundles – Combine multiple products or services into a single bundled offering.

Products

Click on Products to view or manage your list of product offerings.


Click Add to create a new product. Fill in the product details such as name, pricing, and description, then click Save and Add More.


To edit a saved product, click on the product name, make your changes, then click the Update button.


Services

Click on Services to define service-related offerings.


Click Add, enter the service details, and then click Save and Add More to make the service available in the catalog.


To edit a saved service, click on the service name, make your changes, then click the Update button.


Subscriptions

Select Subscriptions to manage recurring revenue items.


Click Add, define the subscription name, billing cycle, and pricing. Then click Save and Add More.


To edit a saved subscription, click on the subscription name, make your changes, then click the Update button.


Bundles

Click on Bundles to create or manage bundled offerings combining multiple items.


Click Add, select the desired products or services to include in the bundle, and click Save and Add More.


To edit a saved bundle, click on the bundle name, make your changes, then click the Update button.
