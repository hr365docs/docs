Customer Success Journey

When you click on Customer Success Journey, the list of all customers will be displayed. You can click on any customer name to view their detailed success journey steps.


1. Onboarding

Click on a customer's name to view the onboarding section.

  • Customer Basic Details – Displays customer's name, company, contact details, timezone, personas, etc.



  • Onboard Process – Overview of the onboarding steps and who is responsible for each step.


  • Timeline - The Timeline provides a chronological view of all customer-related activities such as appointments, tasks, emails, and engagement milestones. It helps track the progress of the customer's journey, offering visibility into key actions taken by the customer success team at each stage.



  • Timeline Task – The Timeline Task section allows you to manage and track specific tasks assigned during the customer journey. You can view task details like title, description, start and due dates, priority, and status. Tasks can be filtered, added, or updated to ensure smooth onboarding and engagement follow-up.



  • Click on Add Task, fill in all the details including the status. If the status is set to Completed and the task is saved, it will be shown under the Completed tab.



  • Appointments – Schedule and view onboarding meetings with stakeholders.


    • Click on New Meeting, then click on the Send button to schedule your meeting.


2. Engagement Check

In the Engagement Activities section, click on each activity (e.g., Feedback Appreciation Email, Confirm Onboarding Completion) to send a pre-defined email for that specific purpose. Each activity helps ensure smooth communication and follow-up during the Engagement Check stage of the customer journey.




  • Timeline - The Timeline provides a chronological view of all customer-related activities such as appointments, tasks, emails, and engagement milestones. It helps track the progress of the customer's journey, offering visibility into key actions taken by the customer success team at each stage.


3. Ongoing Engagement

Ongoing Engagement - In the Ongoing Engagement tab, select from follow-up actions like Cross-sell and Upsell or Application Feedback by sending relevant communications. You can also view any Survey Responses received from customers regarding their onboarding experience.


Monthly Newsletters - Click on Monthly Newsletters to view all the newsletters created for upcoming months. You can upload attachments, save changes, and send the newsletter directly from the action icons provided.


4. Pre-Renewal

Use this stage to prepare renewal strategies and customer feedback before contract renewal.


5. Renewal

This section outlines the renewal decision, status, and next steps for the customer.
