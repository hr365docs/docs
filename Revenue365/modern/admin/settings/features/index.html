    Features

    Revenue 365 application features could be customized.

    Organization

    Click on Configure button.


    In this section, you can add your organization’s details. Start by filling in the general information, then click Save and Continue. Next, enter your bank details and click Save and Continue again.



    Finally, add the terms and conditions, then click Add Organization to complete the setup.



Catagories

Click on Configure button.


You can add category details here, along with the corresponding catalog for each category.


Department

Click on Configure button.


You can add department details here, which will be used when creating a new user.


    Custom Fields

    Click on Configure button


    You can add custom fields to projects and user pages from here.

    To add custom fields to the project page, click on configure button, open the custom filed then click on Add button. Select the project as a category type, fill in the other details, and submit it.



    Catalog

    Click on Configure button.


    In the Catalog section, Product and Service IDs are generated using a prefix (such as PRO or SER) and a numeric suffix (e.g., 1001). If you set the suffix to 1000 in the Catalog settings, the IDs will start from PRO-1000 and increase sequentially (e.g., PRO-1001, PRO-1002). This format applies to new entries under Products, Services, Subscriptions, and Bundles.


    Invoice Module

    Click on Configure button.


    The Invoice Module allows users to manage invoicing settings, including configuring invoice numbers with customizable prefixes and suffixes, time and amount rounding options, and payment due terms. Users can also configure templates for invoices and credit notes, ensuring that the invoicing process is tailored to meet specific business needs. The module can be enabled or disabled with a simple toggle switch, providing flexibility and control over billing operations.


    Invoice Template

    There are four types of templates available for the invoice PDF.


    Upload Logo

    Click Upload to add a logo, then click the Save button to apply it.


    The uploaded logo will appear on the Invoice PDF.


    Stage Configuration

    Click on Configure button.


    Use Stage Configuration to define and manage the workflow stages for your processes.


    Source

    Click on Configure button.


    Sources added here will be shown under Basic Details in the Customer Success Journey. Click on Add button to create a new source.


    Lead Source

    Click on Configure button.


    Lead sources added here will help track where leads originate and will be reflected in the Customer Success Journey.


    Payment Terms

    Click on Configure button.


    Here, you can add new payment terms or manage existing ones such as Immediate, Net 03 Days, Net 07 Days, etc.


    Once you add the payment terms click on "Submit" button


    Payment Method

    Click on Configure button


    When configuring payment methods, you can add new options or manage existing ones such as Cheque, UPI, Bank Transfer, etc.


    Once you added new payment method click on "submit" button


    Payment method will be available in Transactions


    Partner Payment Method

    Click on Configure button to manage partner payment methods.


    When configuring partner payment methods, you can add new options or manage existing ones such as Cheque, UPI, Bank Transfer, Wallet, etc.


    Once you have added a new partner payment method, click on the Submit button to save.


    Configured partner payment methods will be available in all partner-related transactions.
