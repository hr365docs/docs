Administration

When you click on Administration, you will see the following four options:

  1. Clients – Manage and view client information.
  2. Partners – Add or modify partner organizations.
  3. Projects – Track and assign projects to clients.
  4. Approval Workflow – Create and manage approval workflows for revenue processes.

Clients

When you click on Client, the client page will open.


Click on Add and complete the general details form. Then, click Save and Continue.


Enter the contact details in the next section, and click Save and Continue.


To upload documents, click on Attachments, add the required files, and click Add Client to finalize the process.


Partners

When you click on Partners, the partner page will open.


Click on Add and fill in the general information of the partner. Then, click Save and Continue.


Enter the contact details in the next step, and click Save and Continue.


To upload relevant documents, click on Attachments, add the necessary files, and click Add Partner to complete the process.


Projects

When you click on Projects, the project page will open.


Click on Add, fill in the project details, and click the Save button to create a new project.


To make changes to an existing project, click on the project name Update the necessary details and click Update to save the changes.


Approval Workflow

Approval Workflow: The application features an approval workflow for every invoice. By default, the admin of the application serves as the first-level approver


To add a new workflow, click "+ New Approval Workflow", fill in the required fields, and click "Add Approver(s)" to select an approver.


In this feature, you can select single or multiple approvers to approve an invoice. You have the option to choose an approver by their name, their role, the user's manager, or the user's manager's manager.


Click the "Submit" button to save the approval workflow.


To edit a saved approval workflow, click on the workflow name, make your changes, then click the "Update" button.


Delegation

    The Delegation feature in Revenue 365 allows administrators and users to temporarily assign their responsibilities to another user. This ensures continuity of the onboarding process when the original approver or responsible user is unavailable.

  • Open the Delegation panel to view or create delegation records.



  • Click on + Add to create a new delegation entry.



  • Administrators can create delegations for any user, while standard users can only delegate their own responsibilities.



  • From the list, choose the delegatee who will temporarily manage tasks or approvals.



  • Define the start date and end date for the delegation period.



  • Use the Active toggle to enable or disable the delegation. Once all details are filled in, click Submit to finalize.
