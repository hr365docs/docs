  • Home Page

      • The Home Page provides access to all key modules of the application. Below is a quick overview of each section:

      1. Transactions: View, add, and manage your financial transactions.
      2. Proforma Invoice: Create draft invoices for estimates or internal approvals.
      3. Quotation: Generate and share quotations with clients before finalizing a deal.
      4. Invoice: Create and manage final billing documents for completed services or product deliveries.
      5. Products: Manage a list of billable products available for quoting or invoicing.
      6. Services: Add or manage services offered by your organization.
      7. Subscriptions: Create and manage recurring billing plans.
      8. Bundles: Combine multiple products or services into packages or predefined solutions.



    Filters & Status: Use filters to track invoice status — such as Paid, Approved, Sent, or Overdue.


    Refresh Icon: Use the refresh option on the dashboard to update data instantly without reloading the page.
