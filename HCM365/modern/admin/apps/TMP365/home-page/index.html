Home Page



Apply Leave: Click on + Apply Leave, it would open an apply leave from, users could request the leave.



Fill in the information as needed.


Click on Submit button for apply to leave.


My leaves

  • All: Status of all leaves are displayed.


  • Draft: Saved leaves would be displayed; users could edit and submit in future.


  • Pending: Users pending leaves are displayed.


  • Rejected: User would be able to see their rejected leaves.


  • Approved: User can view their approved leaves.



My Approvals

This section is visible only for admins , managers and user (who had Pending Leaves for Approve or Reject), from where leave can be approved or rejected.


  • Approve leave: Approve leave shows in manager action section from where manager can reject or approve leave requests (one at go).

  • Comp off(off in lieu): Managers would be able to see all add comp off requests of their employees and could either approve or reject them.

  • Cancellation: Managers would be able to see all the cancelled leave requests of their employees and could approve or reject them.

Calendar

Now, the user will see the Teams Calendar, where they can view leaves based on the selected date.


Then, the user will see three options: My Calendar, Department's Calendar and Organization's Calendar


If the user selects the Department's Calendar option, they will see leaves in the calendar based on their department.


If the user selects the Organization's Calendar option, they will be able to see all leaves across the organization, including which user is on leave and the corresponding leave dates.


If the user selects the My Calendar option, they will be able to see their own leaves that they have applied for.
