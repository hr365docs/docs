      Notification


      Send End of the Week Notification

      The End of the Week Notification feature automatically alerts users when the week is coming to a close, reminding them to submit their timesheets or review their logged hours. This ensures that all time entries are completed accurately and on time, helping maintain accountability and streamline the reporting process.

      Select Day to Send End of the Week Notification

      The Select Day to Send End of the Week Notification feature allows admins to specify the day of the week on which the end-of-week notifications will be sent to users. This customization ensures that reminders are delivered at the most appropriate time, enhancing user compliance with timesheet submissions and improving overall time management within the organization.

      Following Day as another Notification

      Turn On or Off the notification reminder that is sent on the next day to the last working day of the week.

      Send Invoice Mail to client

      The Send Invoice Mail to Client feature allows users to automatically email invoices to clients upon generation, streamlining the billing process and improving communication.

      Timesheet Submission and Approval Notifications

      Turn On or Off the acknowledge notification that is sent to the users on timesheet submission. And the notification that was sent to the users when the timesheet got approved or rejected.

      Auto Approve Notifications

      You can turn On or Off the notification sent to the users when they submit the timesheet of auto-approve projects.

      Tasks Assign Notification

      Turns On or Off the notification that is sent to the users when the task is assigned to them.

      Enable Daily Timesheet Report Notification

      Turns On or Off the notification that is sent to the Project manager when a user track time or submits the daily timesheet.

      Pending Timesheet approval notification to approver

      The Pending Timesheet Approval Notification feature alerts approvers when timesheets are awaiting their review and approval. This ensures timely processing of submitted timesheets, helping to maintain workflow efficiency and accountability within the approval process.

      Send Pending Timesheet Approval Notification

      The Send Pending Timesheet Approval Notification feature alerts approvers about timesheets awaiting their approval. This ensures timely reviews, streamlining the approval process and maintaining workflow efficiency.
