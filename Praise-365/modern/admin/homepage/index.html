  • Home Page

      • The Praise 365 Home Page provides a clean, intuitive, and user-friendly interface designed to facilitate the recognition of individual and team contributions across your organization, fostering a culture of appreciation and engagement.


    Key features include:

    • Navigate between Praise Given and Praise Received to access categorized entries, allowing users to track both the praises they’ve shared and those they’ve received within the organization.


      • Adding Praise: Click + Praise to open the creation panel.


      Select recipients, choose a badge, compose a message, select a background, and click Submit to send the praise.


    • Menu (Three Dots): Allows users to delete individual praise entries from the list.


    • Action Toolbar: Provides options to export praise data, adjust configuration settings, or access help resources.


    • Search Box: Enables filtering of praise entries by recipient name, badge type, or message content.


    Viewing Praise: Click a recipient’s name to view detailed praise information, including badge, message, and sender.


    Praise Received: Click the Praise Received tab to view who has recognized your contributions and the details of each praise.


      The + Add Image feature allows administrators to upload new badge images that can be used when submitting praise. This makes it easy to customize and expand the recognition options available to users.


      After clicking + Add Image, a form appears where you can enter the Image Title and upload the badge icon. Once saved, the new badge becomes available for selection when adding praise.


      The uploaded image will now appear as a selectable badge option during the praise process.
